About

NationalTreasure

NationalTreasure is an initiative whereby a group of aspiring full stack developers have consolidated data on popular US National Parks in a visual manner using interactive charts. We sourced data from data.world and Kaggle and created plots from a detailed collection of 63 U.S. National Parks. Data on each of the 63 U.S. National Parks includes park size, location coordinates, number of annual visitors, among other useful information. Our website divides the parks by three U.S. regions and provide visualizations on the top 5 most frequently visited parks within each region. We also visualize an overlay of all the National Parks using a map.
